Math Questions:
1. If a train travels 120 miles in 2 hours, what is its average speed in miles per hour? Show your work.
2. Solve for x: 3x + 7 = 22. Show each step.
3. What is 15% of 240? Explain your calculation.
4. A rectangle has length 8 cm and width 5 cm. What is its area? Show your reasoning.
5. If 5 apples cost $2.50, how much do 12 apples cost? Show your calculation.
