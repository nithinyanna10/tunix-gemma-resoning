Reasoning Quality Rubric

Score 0-10 based on the following criteria:

1. Logical Flow (0-3 points)
   - 3: Reasoning steps follow a clear, logical progression
   - 2: Mostly logical with minor gaps
   - 1: Some logical steps but significant gaps
   - 0: No clear logical progression

2. Step Clarity (0-3 points)
   - 3: Each step is clearly explained and easy to follow
   - 2: Steps are mostly clear with minor ambiguities
   - 1: Steps are somewhat unclear or confusing
   - 0: Steps are unclear or missing

3. Completeness (0-2 points)
   - 2: All necessary steps are included
   - 1: Most steps included but some gaps
   - 0: Critical steps missing

4. Correctness of Reasoning (0-2 points)
   - 2: All reasoning steps are factually correct
   - 1: Mostly correct with minor errors
   - 0: Significant factual errors in reasoning

Total: Sum of all points (0-10)
