Generic Reasoning Rubric

Score 0-10 based on the following criteria:

1. Understanding (0-2 points)
   - 2: Demonstrates clear understanding of the question/task
   - 1: Shows partial understanding
   - 0: Misunderstands the question/task

2. Reasoning Process (0-3 points)
   - 3: Clear, logical reasoning process
   - 2: Mostly logical with some gaps
   - 1: Some reasoning but significant gaps
   - 0: No clear reasoning process

3. Answer Quality (0-3 points)
   - 3: Answer is correct, complete, and well-supported
   - 2: Answer is mostly correct and supported
   - 1: Answer is partially correct or incomplete
   - 0: Answer is incorrect or not supported

4. Clarity and Coherence (0-2 points)
   - 2: Very clear and coherent throughout
   - 1: Mostly clear with minor issues
   - 0: Unclear or incoherent

Total: Sum of all points (0-10)
