Math Reasoning Rubric

Score 0-10 based on the following criteria:

1. Problem Understanding (0-2 points)
   - 2: Correctly identifies the problem type and key information
   - 1: Partially understands the problem
   - 0: Misunderstands the problem

2. Solution Strategy (0-2 points)
   - 2: Chooses appropriate method/strategy
   - 1: Strategy is partially appropriate
   - 0: Wrong strategy or no clear strategy

3. Step-by-Step Execution (0-3 points)
   - 3: All steps are correct and clearly shown
   - 2: Most steps correct with minor errors
   - 1: Some steps correct but significant errors
   - 0: Major errors in execution

4. Final Answer (0-2 points)
   - 2: Correct answer
   - 1: Close approximation or minor error
   - 0: Incorrect answer

5. Explanation Quality (0-1 point)
   - 1: Clear explanation of why each step is taken
   - 0: Lacks explanation or unclear

Total: Sum of all points (0-10)
